Beaker is cool

Beaker is an experimental peer-to-peer browser with support for the dat:// protocol. It's by Blue Link Labs, a team of developers who love the Web. They believe the Web is at its best when it empowers creation and encourages participation. That's why they're building a browser for a person-to-person Web. The Beaker team uses peer-to-peer technology to improve how we all can

  • create
  • share
  • connect

on the web. Take the tour. Ready to get started? Beaker is available on desktop for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
